We are excited to invite all EUR researchers to the Programming Café at Erasmus University Rotterdam! Whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting your programming journey, this event is designed to bring together enthusiasts from all levels in a relaxed and collaborative environment.


What can you expect?

In the Programming Café (Code together, feel empowered), you can enhance your programming skills through practical experience and meet like-minded individuals and potential collaborators. Use this opportunity to discover new tools and techniques that can aid your research!

Hands-On Workshops: Dive into interactive sessions tailored for both beginners and advanced programmers. Learn new skills, explore different programming languages, and tackle exciting challenges.

Networking Opportunities: Connect with fellow students, researchers, and staff who share your passion for programming. Exchange ideas, collaborate on projects, and expand your professional network.

Expert Guidance: Benefit from the expertise of our experienced trainers.

Relaxed Atmosphere: Enjoy a casual setting where you can work at your own pace, sip coffee, snack, and engage in stimulating conversations.

Topic/Theme

Each Programming Café has a specific topic or theme. We will offer a short presentation or demonstration (max 1h) about the chosen topic at the beginning of the Café. Interested in something specific? Please send an email to Helena Wedig!


Typical programme

  • 10:50-11:00 Walk-in
  • 11:00-12:00 Presentation or Live Coding
  • 12:00-12:15 Break & Lunch
  • 12:15-13:00 Time to collaborate, ask question and program yourself


Who can join the Programming Café?

The event is open to researchers and PhD students from Erasmus University Rotterdam.
